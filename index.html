💪Tournament Members💪

This competition faces two teams, Europe and the rest of the world. Each team consists of 6 players including 2 chosen by the captain. In all these players we can find the best players in the world like Rafael Nadal, Roger federer or Alexander Zverev

🌍History🌍

The history of the tornament is:The first edition took place in 2017 at the o2 Arena in Prague since this tournament takes place every year in different places around the world
